Frequently asked questions

When should we consult a fertility specialist?

If you've been trying to conceive for 12 months (or 6 months if the woman is over 35), it's advisable to seek expert evaluation.

Will both partners need testing?

Yes. Fertility concerns may involve male factors, female factors, or both. Evaluation helps determine the most appropriate treatment.

Is IVF the first treatment option?

Not always. Depending on your diagnosis, treatment may begin with lifestyle changes, medication, IUI, or other fertility treatments before IVF is recommended.

How long does IVF treatment take?

The treatment timeline varies based on your personalised plan. Your fertility specialist will explain each stage during your consultation.

Are fertility consultations confidential?

Absolutely. All consultations and medical information remain strictly confidential.
