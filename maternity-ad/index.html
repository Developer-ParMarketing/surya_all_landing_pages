Good to know before you book

When should I book my maternity package?

Ideally during the second trimester, to enjoy exclusive early-booking benefits and ensure continuity of care.

Is painless delivery available?

Yes. Our obstetricians offer epidural-assisted painless delivery based on your medical condition.

Can Surya Hospitals manage high-risk pregnancies?

Yes. Our experienced specialists provide comprehensive care for both routine and high-risk pregnancies, including 24×7 Level IV NICU support.

Does Surya Hospitals have a NICU?

Yes. We have a Level IV NICU with experienced neonatologists available 24×7.

Can I choose between normal delivery and C-section?

Your doctor will guide you based on your health and your baby's condition, ensuring the safest delivery option.
